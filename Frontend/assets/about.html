📄 About JurisGenie

1 Our Mission

JurisGenie was created with one clear vision: to make legal language simple, accessible, and actionable. We are the world's first legal infrastructure platform that transforms unstructured contracts into deterministic state machines.

Our platform utilizes advanced 8-layered intelligence to highlight risks, explain obligations, and summarize contracts in plain language — providing safe, scalable, and immutable clarity for the modern digital economy.

Privacy-first AI-powered Actionable

2 Why World Use This

In an increasingly complex global economy, cross-border agreements and digital contracts have become the norm. Subtle clauses in international freelance agreements, SaaS terms, or rental contracts can lead to significant legal exposure. JurisGenie provides global clarity — highlighting risks and suggesting actions in plain, universal language.

Worldwide, billions of deterministic interactions are governed by legal legalese that most people cannot parse. JurisGenie bridges this gap with AI-powered infrastructure, providing a "legal compiler" for every individual and enterprise globally.

3 What We Do

JurisGenie brings together three core capabilities to transform how you interact with legal documents:

⚖️
Clause Analysis
Every clause is broken down and explained in plain language, so you know exactly what you're agreeing to.
🚨
Risk Alerts
High-risk clauses are flagged with color-coded severity scores so nothing slips under the radar.
✅
Actionable Summary
Get a clear executive summary with suggested actions — ready to act on, not just read.

4 How It Works

  • Upload your legal document — PDF, DOCX, or plain text supported
  • Advanced 8-layered scanning assigns risk scores based on global legal standards
  • Receive a deterministic, plain-language executive summary within seconds
  • Ask follow-up questions via the built-in JurisGenie chat assistant
  • Blockchain-ready: prepare your contracts for immutable anchoring
  • Documents are processed securely and never stored without your consent

5 Privacy Commitment

Your documents are yours. JurisGenie processes all content securely and does not store, share, or use your documents for training purposes. Privacy is not a feature — it's a foundation.
