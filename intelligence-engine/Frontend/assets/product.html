📦 Your Contract, Made Simple

JurisGenie turns complex legal documents into clear, human-friendly insights — so you can make confident decisions without needing a law degree.

Upload any legal document and receive a plain-language analysis in seconds. No legal background required. Upload a Document →

1 Key Features

JurisGenie is built around three core capabilities that make legal documents accessible to everyone.

⚖️
Clause-by-Clause Explanations
Every clause is simplified so you know exactly what you're signing — no legal jargon, no guesswork.
🚨
Risk Alerts
Unfair or one-sided clauses are flagged with color-coded severity scores so nothing slips under the radar.
💬
Interactive Intelligence
Ask questions about your document and get instant answers in simple, plain language — like having a legal friend.

2 How It Works

Getting a full legal analysis takes less than a minute. Here's the process:

1
Upload Your Document
Support for PDF, DOCX, and plain text. Simply drag and drop or browse your files.
2
AI Scans Every Clause
Our AI reads the entire document, assigns risk scores, and flags potentially problematic language.
3
Receive Your Analysis
A plain-language executive summary is generated with clarity scores, time saved, and overall risk rating.
4
Ask Follow-up Questions
Use the built-in chat assistant to drill into specific clauses, obligations, or concerns.

3 Who Is It For?

JurisGenie is designed for anyone who deals with legal documents — which is almost everyone.

🎓
Students
Understand hostel agreements, internship contracts, and education loan documents before you sign.
💼
Freelancers
Protect yourself with clear knowledge of client contracts, NDAs, and service agreements.
🏪
Small Businesses
Easily review vendor agreements, commercial leases, and supplier contracts without legal fees.
🚀
Startups

4 Privacy & Security

Your documents are processed securely and are never stored, shared, or used for model training without your explicit consent. JurisGenie is built privacy-first from the ground up.
